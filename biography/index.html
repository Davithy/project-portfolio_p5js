Hello!


Aron Ferreras is an illustrative artist that creates with the aim of breathing life into the characters he makes. He primarily works in digital illustrations, though he occasionally experiments with animations and traditional mediums like paint, charcoal, and pencil sketches. Inspired by the many people he’s met, he creates art striving to tell stories through his work.
